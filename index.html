About UPB

The University Program Board (UPB) is a student-run, student funded organization receiving more funding from the Student Government Association than any other student group. Events offered include concerts, movies, speakers, novelty acts, and many more which are open to ALL students.

  • Campus programming for students, by students
  • Completely free for students (except the Fall Homecoming Concert)
  • 4 unique programming committees that gives students professional experience
  • Leadership and professional growth oppurtunities

Spirit & Traditions (SNT) cooridantes traditional campus events, including Weeks of Welcome programming, that focus on the first-year student expierence. Events that SNT offers include:
  • Late Night Breakfast
  • Festival Fun Fair
  • Bridgeforth Stadium Movie
  • First Day of Classes Palooza

Amplify brings live entertainment events to JMU's campus through an external promotion company, finding local acts, JMU students, and collaborations with other organizations. Events that Amplify offers include:
  • Fall Concert
  • Mad Laughs
  • Purple Reign
  • Battle of the Bands

Community Engagement creates events that engage with other organizations within JMU and the Harrisonburg community. This committee creates inclusive environments where students can find their own community and feel welcome on campus. Events that Commuinty Engagement offers include:
  • Henna Hour
  • Bookmarks and Bubbles
  • Cards for Kids

Special Events (SPEV) coordiantes smaller signature UPB programming each month, and focuses on creating unqiue experiences students can't find oustide of campus. Events that SPEV offers include:
  • JMZoo
  • Tie Blanket Making
  • Monthly Bingo Nights
  • Goat Yoga

Events we offer

UPB hosts a wide variety of events on campus, ranging from small scale crafting events to sold out concerts inside the Atlantic Union Bank Center. With a such a diverse student body, UPB strives to provide events for every student's interests.

With having so many events on campus, its hard to keep track of them all. A more comprehensive list of events that UPB has put on, and will be putting on, can be found on our JMU BeInvolved page

Traditional events we offer include:

  • The Fall Concert
    • Each fall we bring a national touring artist to campus! This concert takes place in the beautiful AUBC Auditorium, so tickets are limited. As the show comes closer, more information will become available on who we are bringing to campus and ticket sale information.
  • Monthly Late Night Breakfasts
    • Late Night Breakfast is a fun event for those who love having meals at unconventional times! Students come to enjoy not only breakfast foods, but great music, crafts, photo booths and so much more. We even keep the party going until midnight!
  • Special Events
    • Special Events are unique events which aim to provide, entertaining and educational events for our campus and community. Special events open for all students, striving to accomplish and cultivate new ideas that have never been done before at JMU. Whether you’re a fan of ghost stories, ice skating rinks, cooking demos, or anything in between, UPB’s Special Events aspires to engage a diverse population of students through its various events.
  • Purple Reign
    • Not ready for the school year to be over? Purple Reign is a spring concert and carnival outside on Festival Lawn. Come enjoy activities, food, and great music as you celebrate the end of the semester with your friends!

Become a Member

Want to be more than an attendee? You can be a participant in the event planning process! UPB is staffed by students who coordinate booking, promoting, and producing programs for students. By joining UPB, students have the opportunity to determine performers, artists, and acts who will educate and entertain JMU students. Members receive the inside scoop on campus events and work as event coordinators who have a BLAST doing their job.

You’ll get the chance to work on exciting committees such as Spirit & Traditions, Amplify, Community Engagement, and Special Events. You will live, learn, and create memorable experiences for the JMU community while meeting a highly motivated, like-minded group of individuals.

Interested in joining? Follow our Instagram page @upbjmu and click the link in our bio to joing our interest Groupme! Further instructions will be provided once in the Groupme. Don't have Instagram? Don't worry! Come to one of our events and talk to a leadership memeber and they will be able to add you into the Groupme!
